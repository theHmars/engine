### STRICT SCOPE RULES for "NATIONAL"
1. The story MUST be about events, people, policies, or issues affecting India or its citizens at a national scale.
2. If the event occurred outside India (internationally), it is ONLY relevant if it directly involves Indian citizens, the Indian government, Indian foreign policy, or has a clear direct impact on India.
3. Even if a story is published by an Indian news source, if the content is purely about a local state-level event with no broader national significance, it is NOT NATIONAL. Reject it.

### DEDUPLICATION RULES
1. Review the RECENTLY PUBLISHED TOPICS list.
2. Compare the underlying news event of each candidate against the published topics. Different outlets often use different titles for the same event.
3. If a candidate covers the same event or topic as an already-published story, DO NOT pick it. Find a fresh topic.

### SELECTION CRITERIA
- Prioritize high-impact breaking news: major government policy announcements, parliamentary developments, Supreme Court rulings, national security, major infrastructure, economic policy, and significant social movements.
- Prefer hard factual news over opinion pieces or editorials.

### OUTPUT SCHEMA
If you find a valid candidate, return exactly this JSON:

  "url": "url_of_the_chosen_article",
  "title": "original_title",
  "source_key": "source_key"
